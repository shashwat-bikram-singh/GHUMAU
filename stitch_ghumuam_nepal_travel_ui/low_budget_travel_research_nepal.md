# Research Results: Low Budget Travel in Nepal (2026)

Based on the provided image and web research, here are the detailed low-budget options for Nepal.

## 1. Dhulikhel & Namobuddha (Budget: NPR 4800-8000)
- **Must Visit:** Thrangu Tashi Yangtse Monastery, Namobuddha Stupa, Sunrise views, nature trails.
- **Hotels:** Namobuddha Monastic Guest House (High value/spiritual), local lodges in Dhulikhel.
- **Budget Tip:** Stay in the monastery guest house for a spiritual experience and lower costs compared to luxury resorts.

## 2. Ilam (Kanyam & Antu Danda) (Budget: NPR 4800-8000)
- **Must Visit:** Kanyam Tea Gardens, Antu Danda (earliest sunrise in Nepal), Pathibhara temple.
- **Hotels:** Antu Community Homestays, Hill Tower.
- **Budget Tip:** Ilam is a cheaper, less crowded alternative to Nagarkot for sunrise views.

## 3. Tansen (Budget: NPR 4800-8000)
- **Must Visit:** Rani Mahal (the 'Taj Mahal of Nepal'), Srinagar Hill, Amar Narayan Temple.
- **Hotels:** Local lodges, Srinagar Hotel (budget annex).
- **Budget Tip:** Access Rani Mahal via public local jeeps to save 70% compared to private hire.

## 4. Nagarkot (Budget: NPR 5500-8000)
- **Must Visit:** Panoramic hiking trail, Nagarkot View Tower.
- **Hotels:** Budget-specific annexes of larger hotels, Country Villa (budget wing).
- **Budget Tip:** Look for "annex" rooms in high-end hotels to get the same views at a fraction of the price.

## 5. Kathmandu (Budget: NPR 6500-8000)
- **Must Visit:** Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple), Boudhanath Stupa, Pashupatinath.
- **Hotels:** Sparkling Turtle Backpackers, Nunnery guesthouses in Boudha, Yala Peak, Nepalaya.
- **Budget Tip:** Use ride-sharing apps like Pathao/InDrive to avoid the "tourist tax" on taxis.

## 6. Bandipur (Budget: NPR 6500-8000)
- **Must Visit:** Siddha Cave (largest cave in Nepal), Newari architecture in the old town, Tundikhel.
- **Hotels:** Community homestays, The Old Inn (budget rooms).
- **Budget Tip:** Eat 'bottomless' Dal Bhat for a fixed-cost nutritional safety net.

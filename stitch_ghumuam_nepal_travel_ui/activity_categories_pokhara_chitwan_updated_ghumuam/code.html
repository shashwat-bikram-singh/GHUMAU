High Altitude $$$

Everest Region

A legendary landscape where earth meets sky. Experience the profound silence of the highest peaks, the resilience of Sherpa culture, and trails that have called to adventurers for generations.

place Must-Visit Places

Namche Bazaar $$

The bustling heart of the Khumbu region, a vital acclimatization stop rich in culture and vibrant markets.

Kala Patthar $$$

The ultimate vantage point offering unparalleled, up-close views of Mount Everest's imposing summit.

Tengboche $$

Home to the largest gompa in the Khumbu, offering spiritual solace against a backdrop of Ama Dablam.

Diverse Terrain $$

Annapurna Region

A journey through shifting ecological zones. From subtropical forests teeming with life to barren, high-altitude alpine deserts, the Annapurna trails are a masterclass in natural diversity.

place Must-Visit Places

Thorong La Pass $$$

The challenging apex of the circuit at 5,416m, rewarding trekkers with an unforgettable sense of achievement.

Poon Hill $

Famous for spectacular, panoramic sunrise views over the entire Annapurna and Dhaulagiri mountain ranges.

Manang $$

A fascinating village in the rain shadow area, featuring unique Tibetan-influenced culture and stark, beautiful landscapes.

Lakes & Mountains $$

Pokhara

The tourist capital of Nepal, known for its serene lakes, stunning views of the Annapurna range, and as a gateway to the famous Annapurna Circuit. NPR 7,000 – 10,000 budget range.

place Must-Visit Places

Phewa Lake $$

Enjoy a peaceful boat ride on Nepal's second-largest lake, reflecting the majestic Machhapuchhre mountain.

Sarangkot $$

Renowned for panoramic sunrise views over the Himalayas and world-class paragliding experiences.

Davis Falls $

A unique waterfall that flows directly into an underground tunnel, surrounded by lush vegetation.

World Peace Pagoda $

A brilliant white stupa situated on a narrow ridge high above Phewa Lake offering spectacular views.

Wildlife Safari $$

Chitwan

A world heritage site famous for its dense jungles, diverse wildlife including the one-horned rhinoceros, and vibrant local culture. NPR 8,000 – 10,000 budget range.

place Must-Visit Places

Jungle Safari $$$

Explore the deep jungle via jeep to witness rare and beautiful animals in their natural habitat.

Canoe Ride $$

Glide along the Rapti River spotting crocodiles, exotic birds, and lush riverine forests.

Wildlife Spotting $$

Keep your eyes peeled for rare sightings including Bengal tigers, leopards, and over 500 species of birds.

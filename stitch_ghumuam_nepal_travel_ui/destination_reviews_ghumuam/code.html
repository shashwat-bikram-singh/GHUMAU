star star star star star
"A piece of heaven on earth"

Pokhara exceeded all my expectations. Waking up to the view of Machapuchare reflecting perfectly in Phewa Lake is a memory I'll cherish forever. The lakeside area is incredibly clean and feels very safe to walk around, even late at night. The balance of nature and comfortable amenities makes it the perfect base before a trek.

David M.

Sydney, AU

Traveled Sep 2023

star star star star star
"Relaxing vibe, great food"

After finishing the Annapurna Base Camp trek, Pokhara was exactly what I needed. The cafe culture here is surprisingly robust, with excellent coffee and bakeries. Renting a scooter to visit the Peace Pagoda is a must-do. Deducting one star only because the main street can get a bit crowded with traffic during peak hours, but stepping down to the lakeside paths instantly solves that.

"Paragliding was unforgettable"

The adventure sports scene here is world-class. I went paragliding from Sarangkot and the views of the valley and lake below, framed by the Himalayas, were breathtaking. The instructors felt very safe and professional. The town itself has a beautiful, slow pace compared to Kathmandu. Truly a sanctuary.
